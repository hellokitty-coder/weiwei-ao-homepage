个人简介 / Biography

中文简介

敖微微，武汉大学数学与统计学院教授，博士生导师、硕士生导师。主要从事非线性偏微分方程、非线性椭圆方程组、奇异摄动问题、反应扩散系统、非局部方程与高阶偏微分方程等方向的研究。研究工作围绕非线性偏微分方程中的集中现象、爆破解、奇异解、斑图形成、Chern–Simons 系统、Toda 系统、分数阶 Yamabe 问题及相关几何分析问题展开。

English Biography

Weiwei Ao is a Professor in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at Wuhan University. Her research interests include nonlinear partial differential equations, concentration phenomena in nonlinear PDEs and elliptic systems, singular perturbation problems, reaction-diffusion systems, and nonlocal and higher-order PDEs.
